About Our School

"Let Your Light Shine"

A legacy of faith, learning and character since 1982.

St. Mary's Convent School, Gajraula is a distinguished Christian minority institution established under the management of the Franciscan Sisters of Our Lady of Graces — a registered Charitable Society under the Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860.

Affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi, we offer a curriculum designed to lead students from foundational years through the Senior Secondary Examination, fostering academic rigour alongside moral and spiritual growth.

Our vision is to be an institution of excellence dedicated to value-based, wholesome education, preparing boys and girls for tomorrow's world — imbued with service, integrity, and compassion in the spirit of Christ's teachings.
